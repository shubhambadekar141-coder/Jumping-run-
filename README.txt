Jumping Run Ultimate Game

Features:
- Endless runner with jump mechanic
- 20 purchasable skins in shop (Bird, Dragon, Unicorn, etc.)
- Shields with temporary protection
- Collect coins and avoid obstacles
- Shop to buy/equip skins and shields
- Cartoon graphics and sound effects (jump, coin, hit, shield, game over)
- Mobile and browser ready

Instructions:
- Tap/click or press spacebar to jump
- Collect coins and avoid obstacles
- Buy skins and shields in the shop
- Equip skins to change character appearance
- Activate shield to survive obstacles temporarily
